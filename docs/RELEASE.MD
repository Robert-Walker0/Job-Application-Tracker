# Job Application Tracker

**What it is:****Stop losing track of where you applied.**

Job hunting is chaotic. Applications pile up across dozens of companies, follow-up dates get forgotten, and it's impossible to remember whether you heard back from that role you applied to three weeks ago.

Job Application Tracker gives you one place to log every application, track its status, and know exactly where you stand at any point in your search.

**What you get:**
- Log every job application in seconds: company, role, apply date, pay type, pay amount, link, platform, last heard from, and notes
- Track status at a glance — Applied, Phone Screen, Interview, Offer, Rejected, Withdrawn
- Filter by status, date, or company to find what you need fast
- Never forget a follow-up — applications sitting in Applied for more than two weeks get flagged automatically

**Who it's for:**
Anyone actively job hunting who wants clarity over chaos, especially self-taught developers and career switchers managing high application volumes with no recruiter help.

A lightweight web application. No install required, runs in your browser, free to use.
Optionally, you can run it yourself by following the instructions in the root README.md.
